Jollof Rice: A Delicious Delight

This savory and spicy jollof rice recipe is a celebration of West African flavours.

Packed with a rich blend of tomatoes, peppers, and aromatic spices, every spoonful of this dish offers a burst of vibrant and tantalizing taste

Ingredients

  • long grain paraboiled rice
  • tomatoes
  • bell pepper
  • scoth bonnet
  • onions
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • chicken
  • seasoning and herbs

Steps to Make

  1. Saute the onions
  2. Stir the tomatoe paste
  3. Add ginger and garlic along with tomato paste
  4. Add blended peppers
  5. Add thyme, curry powder, salt, white pepper, and seasoning cubes
  6. Stir the rice
  7. Add chicken stock
  8. Continue cooking until the rice is done
