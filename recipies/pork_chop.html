Porck Chops: A Perfect Blend of Sweet and Savory

Ingredients

  • Pork chop
  • OLive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps to Make

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Pat pork chops dry with paper towels
  3. Rub pork chops with olive oil, season both sides with salt and pepper
  4. Heat a skillet over high heat. Once hot, add a bit of oil to the skillet
  5. Place pork chops over the skillet and sear each side for 3 - 4 minutes
  6. Transfer pork chops to a baking dish
  7. Bake epork chops for about 10 - 15 minutes
  8. Remove pork chops from oven and let them rest before serving
